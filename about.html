Dr. B Rajasekhar


Dr. B Rajasekhar is a highly qualified physician specializing in Internal/General Medicine. He obtained his medical degree from Bangalore Medical College and Research Institute, Bangalore. Dr. Rajasekhar has a wealth of experience, having served as a consultant in several reputable medical institutions such as Shirdi Sai Hospital, Ulsoor (2006-2012), Apoorva Diagnostic (2012-2014), Manipal Cure and Care Clinic, Koramangala (2012-present), Apollo Clinic, Indiranagar (2013-2014), Shyamala Hospital, Ulsoor (2011-present), and Health Cottage Hospital, Ulsoor (2006-present). He is registered with the Karnataka Medical Council (KMC) under registration number 26,265,1987.

Currently, Dr. Rajasekhar is the owner of Six Face Clinic, where he provides exceptional medical care to patients of all ages, both men and women. He adopts a diversified and progressive approach to his practice, and offers a wide range of services including diagnostics and treatments, as well as minor procedures with the consent of his patients. He is based in Indiranagar/Ulsoor, Bangalore and his consultation fee is Rs. 600.

With his extensive knowledge and experience, Dr. Rajasekhar is committed to providing high-quality, professional medical care to his patients. He is dedicated to helping his patients achieve optimal health and well-being.
